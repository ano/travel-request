Step 1: Request Itenrary

Contact your travel agent and request an itinerary to be created for your travel destinations. Once this has been emailed through fill in your the travel requisition form.

Fill in your travel details and use your itinerary to fill in the travel request form. Make sure to also upload a copy of your itinerary.

Your travel request will then go to the relevant managers for approval. If you are a manager you can login and approve users.
